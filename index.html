About Us

"Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses."

toptrace range

Toptracer Range is a cutting-edge technology system that transforms the traditional driving range experience. It employs camera-based tracking to monitor the flight of a golf ball, displaying its trajectory in real time and providing instant feedback on every shot1. This innovation has revolutionized how golf is viewed on TV and is now enhancing golfers’ driving range practice sessions.
